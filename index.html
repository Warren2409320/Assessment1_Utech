AI Business Idea Report (2025)

Favourite Idea: AI-Powered Personal Finance Coach

The idea is an AI assistant that helps people plan budgets, track spending, and make smarter financial decisions. In my world, this could help students and working adults in Jamaica manage bills, savings, tuition planning, and business cash flow with clearer guidance.

Why it would be useful

  • Encourages consistent budgeting habits with reminders and simple goals.
  • Helps users understand spending patterns using dashboards and summaries.
  • Supports small business owners with basic cash-flow tracking and planning.

Ethical issues & my solutions

  • Privacy: Financial data is sensitive. Solution: data minimization, encryption, and clear user consent.
  • Bias / poor advice: AI can make wrong assumptions. Solution: show explanations, allow user corrections, and recommend human review for major decisions.
  • Over-reliance: People may follow AI blindly. Solution: add warnings, education tips, and “confidence level” indicators.

Real World Example

One example is Rocket Money, which helps people track subscriptions and spending.
